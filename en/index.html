Film & event logistics in France — parking, security & convoy

BMS Ventouse is your local partner in France for parking neutralization (ventousage), on‑set security & guarding and vehicle convoy for film, TV, advertising, fashion and brand events. Based near Paris, we operate 24/7 with fast, production‑friendly coordination.

What we do for international productions

We act as your local logistics and street control team in France. We work with line producers, location managers, production managers and fashion/event agencies to secure the street, manage access and keep your schedule under control.

Parking neutralization (ventousage)

We secure reserved parking and clear streets for trucks, talent and tech vehicles: temporary no‑parking signs, AOT / filming permits, resident notifications and on‑site crew (ventouseurs) to keep the perimeter free.

Details (EN): Parking neutralization in Paris & France

On‑set security & guarding

24/7 on‑set security teams to protect equipment, sets and talent, manage access and confidentiality around your shoot or event, in coordination with your production and local authorities.

Vehicle & set convoy

Convoy of technical vehicles, picture cars and sets between Paris, studios and regional locations, with experienced drivers aligned with your parking plan and call sheet.

More details (FR): convoyage véhicules & décors

BMS Ventouse has supported projects ranging from French series to advertising campaigns and large public events, including operations linked to major international sports events in France. We are used to high‑stakes, time‑sensitive shoots with many stakeholders.

Fashion, advertising & brand events

We regularly support fashion shows, showrooms, runway rehearsals and editorial shoots in Paris, including during Paris Fashion Week. We secure parking, traffic flow and backstage access so your creative teams can focus on the show.

Our core base is Paris / Île‑de‑France, but we operate in all major French cities: Lyon, Marseille, Bordeaux, Lille, Nice, Strasbourg, Toulouse and more. For some projects, we can also support shoots that involve France–Belgium moves.
