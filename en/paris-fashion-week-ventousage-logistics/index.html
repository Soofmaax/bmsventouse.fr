Paris Fashion Week – parking neutralization & event logistics

We secure parking, access and street logistics for shows, rehearsals and shootings during Paris Fashion Week: parking neutralization (ventousage), access for trucks, resident notifications and on‑site crew so that streets stay usable for your production, not random traffic.

Paris Fashion Week 2026 – key dates

We follow the official Paris Fashion Week calendar published by the Fédération de la Haute Couture et de la Mode (FHCM). For 2026, the main periods are:

  • Menswear Autumn/Winter 2026–2027: 20–25 January 2026
  • Womenswear Autumn/Winter 2026–2027: 2–10 March 2026

Around these dates, many brands also plan off‑calendar shows, presentations, look‑book and campaign shootings. We adapt to both official calendar and off‑schedule projects.

What we handle for fashion shows & shootings

Parking & street control

  • Reserved parking bays for trucks, shuttles, cars and talent vehicles
  • Temporary no‑parking signs and cones (ventousage)
  • Resident notifications and photo proof
  • On‑site crew to keep the perimeter clear before and during the event

Access & security perimeter

  • Coordination with your own security teams and venue
  • Safe walkways and access routes for guests, VIPs and staff
  • Perimeter marking around backstage and technical areas
  • Adaptation to local constraints (narrow streets, neighbours, traffic)

Logistics for shootings

  • Parking and access for photo and video crews
  • Support for look‑book / campaign shootings in the city
  • Coordination with convoy and technical vehicles
  • Multi‑day arrangements when you move from one location to another
